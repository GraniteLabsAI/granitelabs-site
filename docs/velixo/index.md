## <span class="cube-icon cube-storage"></span> Overview

Velixo is the reporting layer for Acumatica, providing Excel-based real-time financial and operational reporting.

This section documents how Velixo is used to extract, structure, and present Acumatica data in a consistent and repeatable way.

---

## 🎯 Objective

- Provide structured Velixo reporting guidance  
- Support financial and operational reporting  
- Improve data visibility and usability  
- Document real-world reporting patterns  

---

## 🧠 Key Concepts

- Excel-based reporting  
- Real-time Acumatica data connection  
- Generic Inquiry (GI) usage  
- Project and job cost reporting  
- Financial reporting structure

## ⚠️ Notes

- Velixo is a reporting layer, not a data source  
- Accuracy depends on Acumatica configuration  
- Reports should be structured and repeatable  
- Avoid one-off or manual report designs
